Dungeon Teleports Updated for 11.2.0
Key Features:

Dungeon Teleport Buttons: Adds intuitive teleport buttons directly to your Mythic+ Dungeon panel for each dungeon portal you've unlocked.
Easy Access: Quickly teleport to any dungeon entrance saving time and effort.
Auto-Detection:  Detects which portals you have unlocked and only displays the relevant buttons.
Minimal Resource Usage: Designed to be lightweight and efficient, choose hide options to reduce animations or hide known portals.
